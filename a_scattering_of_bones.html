Mary O'Reilly Paranormal Mysteries: A Scattering of Bones (Kindle Worlds Novella)

When asked by a former colleague to help her daughter, a young girl being haunted by a frightening ghost, Mary has no idea that the mystery she stumbles into will quickly become entangled with the case that has just landed on Bradley Alden’s desk: an assortment of bones in an unmarked grave. Feeling at a loss now that Mike has moved on, Mary nevertheless works diligently to uncover the story behind a ghost—or two— who are terrified to talk to the police, even an ex-cop like Mary. Luckily, Bradley is right beside her when the story she seeks somehow leads back to a scattering of bones.
