Hunting Spirits

A warm summer night. A group of lifelong pals. The excitement of a risky adventure. The life-threatening touch of a malevolent spirit.

AJ Podalak knows he made a mistake the night he went to the cemetery with his friends and watched as two of them tried to contact the dead with the use of a Ouija board. Now each of them is in serious condition in the hospital, having been picked off one by one by a vengeful spirit, leaving AJ the last of the group still walking around. Can he turn the tables and hunt down this spirit with the help of a famous ghost hunter's daughter, or is it already too late for him?
