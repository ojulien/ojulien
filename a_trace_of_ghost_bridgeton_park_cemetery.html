A Trace of Ghost (A Bridgeton Park Cemetery Book)

For clarity of content, the author suggests reading the Bridgeton Park Cemetery books before reading this novella.

Ghost story night brings a blast from Cassie Valentine’s past that Michael Penfield is unprepared to deal with, but deal with it he must as a story of a friend’s haunting leads to one family’s tragedy from the past. Cassie is keen to help out a friend and drags Michael, willing or no, into the process…
