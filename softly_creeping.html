Softly Creeping: A Modern Collection of Penny Dreadfuls

Creatures, Hauntings, Legends, Myths, Phenomenon. All things that make us think, make us wonder, and sometimes makes our hair stand on end. Visit the realm of the unknown, where mysterious creatures lurk in the shadows. A place where myths and legends abound. Take in the stories and ponder their meaning. Read about unexplained phenomenon, and wonder.

Three authors offer glimpses into the question, what if? Some of the stories are dark, some are scary, and some are hopeful. But all these stories have a common thread… the unknown.
